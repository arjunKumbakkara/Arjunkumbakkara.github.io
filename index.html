Profile

I'm a Senior Tech Lead specialized in Artificial Intelligence from Bengaluru,INDIA with work experience in Europe(BeNeLux).I believe ,there exists nothing more euphoric and fulfilling as the very process of creation or rather building something.A solution well placed is a creation in itself.

From writing code for my first Android app in a small startup garage to architecting cutting-edge AI systems for enterprise telecom giants, my journey has been one of relentless evolution. I began as a curious engineer with a passion for crafting meaningful solutions, co-founding Dexideate Technologies in 2015 to build SaaS and mobile tools for local businesses. After earning my B.E. in Computer Science, I joined 6D Technologies in 2017, where I grew from a backend developer into a seasoned tech lead, spearheading mission-critical systems across mediation, IoT, and SIM lifecycle management. My work modernized legacy Java architectures into cloud-native microservices, enabling scalable, high-availability platforms for global telecom operations. But I didn’t stop there. Fueled by a deep curiosity for intelligence beyond rules and logic, I pursued advanced AI education—earning an M.Sc. in Artificial Intelligence from Liverpool John Moores University, with a research thesis on carbon-efficient transformer models for code generation that earned faculty recognition. Today, I specialize in blending domain expertise with AI innovation—building GenAI-powered assistants for SIM provisioning, charging systems, roaming intelligence, and telecom workflows. My focus lies in retrieval-augmented generation, LLM optimization, and end-to-end MLOps, grounded in real-world use cases. This portfolio is not just a showcase of my work—it’s a testament to a decade-long mission: to bridge enterprise-scale engineering with human-centric artificial intelligence, creating systems that feel intuitive, insightful, and indispensable.

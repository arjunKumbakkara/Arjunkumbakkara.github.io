NewsSnap

Category : Social Apps/Productivity
Client : Non-Disclosure
Description : AI-Powered Personalized News Digest
Role : Technical Architect / Lead Developer

NewsSnap is an AI-driven mobile and web app that delivers concise, hyper-personalized news summaries tailored to your interests, time, and reading style. Leveraging advanced generative AI and user behavior analytics, NewsSnap filters through thousands of daily articles and curates bite-sized news digests just for you—making staying informed effortless and engaging. Key Features: • AI-generated real-time summaries from trusted news sources • Personalized feeds based on reading behavior and preferences • Smart categorization (Politics, Tech, Business, Entertainment, etc.) • Multi-language support with sentiment highlights • Voice-read summaries and push notifications for breaking stories Architecture Highlights: • News ingestion from APIs and RSS feeds • Summarization using transformer-based GenAI models (T5, GPT, BART) • User preference modeling with collaborative filtering • Mobile-first front-end with dark/light mode and offline caching Use Cases: • Busy professionals needing news on the go • Students tracking current affairs • Executives preparing for meetings • Anyone seeking quick, AI-curated updates
